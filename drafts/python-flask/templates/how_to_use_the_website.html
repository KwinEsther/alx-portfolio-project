How to Use this Habit Tracker

Getting Started

To track your habits, add daily habits you want to build, such as exercising, drinking water, reading, etc., and mark them accordingly as you go.

Example:

Habits Day 1 Day 2 Day 3 Day 4 Day 5 Day 6 Day 7
Exercise ✅ ❌ ❌ ✅ ✅ ✅ ✅
Read ❌ ❌ ❌ ✅ ✅ ❌ ❌
Spanish class ✅ ✅ ✅ ✅ ✅ ✅ ✅

Using Small Steps

1. Guest Mode

  • Use the app without signing up to test how it works.
  • Note: Your data won't be saved. If you leave or refresh the page, your progress will be lost.

2. Sign Up to Save Your Progress

  1. Create a free account to save your habits and track your journey over time.
  2. After signing up, you can log in anytime to view, update, or add new habits.

3. Take Control of Your Journey

Stay consistent and work toward your goals with this simple, effective tracker designed to keep you motivated.
